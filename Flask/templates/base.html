Rock Identification Using Deep Convolution Neural Network

Identifying the types of rocks


Rocks are a fundamental component of Earth. The automatic identification of rock type in the field would aid geological surveying, education, and automatic mapping.The traditional analysis is time taken process to identify the rocks.Using deep convolutional neural networks we can accurately identify the rock types based on image analysis.
